Our Mission

SYM is a non-profit organization aiming to give middle school and high school students in SLO county opportunities to interact with people from diverse backgrounds whom they would be less likely to interact with in daily life through volunteering.

“I’m from a school where I am surrounded by people growing up in similar cultural backgrounds to mine. Volunteering with SYM forced me out of my comfort zone and I’ve gained valuable experience through the events.”

-SYM Volunteer
